# Investment Memo Template

This template is used to present proposed product or technology investments to portfolio governance leadership.

The memo provides a structured framework for evaluating strategic alignment, expected value, delivery risk, and capital allocation tradeoffs.

---

## Investment Summary

**Initiative Name**

Short descriptive title of the initiative.

**Investment Owner**

Product, engineering, or cross-functional leader responsible for the proposal.

**Strategic Theme Alignment**

Which enterprise strategic theme or objective the initiative supports.

**Investment Type**

- Strategic
- Growth
- Efficiency
- Risk Reduction
- Platform / Infrastructure

---

## Problem Statement

Describe the problem or opportunity that motivates the investment.

Questions typically addressed:

- What problem are we solving?
- Why does it matter now?
- What happens if we do nothing?

---

## Proposed Solution

Describe the proposed initiative.

Include:

- product or platform capabilities to be delivered
- affected systems or services
- expected timeline or delivery phases

---

## Strategic Alignment

Explain how this initiative supports enterprise strategy.

Example considerations:

- strategic differentiation
- regulatory or mission requirements
- customer value
- operational resilience

---

## Expected Outcomes

Define the measurable outcomes expected from the investment.

Examples:

- revenue growth
- cost reduction
- customer adoption
- delivery predictability improvements

Where possible, identify metrics that will be used to evaluate success.

---

## Investment Size

Provide an estimate of the required investment.

Typical dimensions include:

- engineering capacity
- product management capacity
- infrastructure or platform costs
- external vendor or partner costs

---

## Delivery Risk Assessment

Identify potential risks that could impact delivery success.

Examples:

- technical complexity
- cross-team dependencies
- regulatory constraints
- integration challenges

Provide an overall qualitative risk assessment:

```
Low
Moderate
High
```

---

## Portfolio Impact

Explain how this initiative affects the broader product or technology portfolio.

Consider:

- competing investments
- sequencing constraints
- resource tradeoffs
- strategic prioritization implications

---

## Alternatives Considered

Summarize alternative approaches that were evaluated.

Explain why the proposed approach was selected.

---

## Recommendation

Summarize the investment recommendation for the governance body.

Possible outcomes:

- Approve investment
- Approve with modifications
- Defer
- Reject

---
